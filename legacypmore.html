Legacy

Our Principal Sir

Dr. M. Jagapathi Raju is currently offering his services as Principal of SRKR Engineering College. He's been teaching for more than 35 years. He earned a bachelor's degree in civil engineering from Bangalore University and a master's degree in highway and traffic engineering from India's premier and prestigious IIT Kharagpur. He commenced his teaching career as a lecturer in 1983. Over the years, he has worked as a Reader, Professor, Head of Department, and Vice-Principal of SRKR Engineering College.

He also served as Principal at West Godavari Institute of Science and Engineering and MNR College of Engineering, Sangareddy. He is also the chairman of the SRKR Engineering College's Academic Council and Combined Board of Studies. On the eve of Dr. Sarvepalli Radha Krishnan Jayanthi Celebrations in 2019, he was awarded The Best Teacher Award in recognition of his selfless dedication towards teaching and students. He has framed R19, R17, and R16 syllabi for the concerned students and established 9 startups as a Principal.

SHOW LESS

SRKR ISTE received the Best Students Chapter Award at the ISTE National Convention in 2017 at the Regional Institute of Science and Technology, Technocity, Meghalaya, and again in 2018 at the ISTE National Convention in Ahmedabad. SRKR ISTE hosts a national level techno management symposium called "NIPUNA," which has been held since 2007 with the goal of improving the skills of students in the fields of business, communication, presentation, critical and creative thinking, decision making, problem solving, art, and technology.

NIPUNA is one of the most remarkable techno-management symposiums with an electrifying set of events which include various activities that test student's core and technical knowledge along with their managerial skills. Students from various colleges attend to have a spectacular experience that lasts forever. It also gives them a chance to self evaluate their performances in extra curricular activities like art, speaking, writing, and many more. It provides a platform for students to enhance their technical and non-technical prowess with a diversity of events that are a perfect amalgamation of technology and management.

Our FA Sir

The Faculty Advisor plays a crucial role in directing the team to imagine things out of the box and plan them with accuracy. Under the guidance of the Faculty Advisor, Team ISTE never fails to learn discipline, punctuality, authenticity, planning, management, and goal orientation. As a Faculty Advisor, Mr. P. Subbarao provided his never ending support and remarkable guidance which led the team to win The Best Student Chapter Award in the two consecutive years, 2017 and 2018.

Regardless of the priority of the task, he works with utmost sincerity and honesty and this has aided ISTE in achieving perfection. He fulfilled both the roles of Faculty Advisor and Professor with the same dedication and determination. ISTE is very fortunate to have him as the Faculty Advisor who stands with us at all the times and extends his helping hand in every difficulty we encounter in our journey. He is undoubtedly the driving force behind ISTE's stellar image and backbone for the indisputable legacy of SRKR ISTE.
