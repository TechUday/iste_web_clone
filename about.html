Meet the crew!

Senior Body Members (SBMs) are the former Executive Body Members (EBMs) who have dedicated their efforts and hard work to uphold the great legacy of SRKR ISTE. They crossed an extra mile in every possible way, allocating so much of their valuable time to improve the reputation and to produce high-quality, efficient results. They showed the world who they are and what they are capable of through their coordination and smart work. After successfully accomplishing all of the tremendous tasks as EBMs, they take the new responsibility of SBMs to guide Executive Body Members towards the proper path that leads them to the right destination. They introduce them to a new world that is filled with exceptional opportunities and encourage them to learn new skills which are of great value in the future. SBMs do all their best to help EBMs discover their innate potentials and channel them in the most successful way that also upticks the name and fame of ISTE.
