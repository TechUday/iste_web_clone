Meet the crew!

Executive Body Members are the students from the penultimate year of graduation working for SRKR ISTE. Students are selected for these vital roles after filtering through a series of written tests and personal interviews. In SRKR ISTE, EBMs unite as a team to work together in planning and effectively organizing various types of activities and events with a primary goal of encouraging students to develop their communication and presentation skills, overcome their stage fear, and provide a platform where they can interact and share their ideas with students from other departments. During this period, EBMs open doors for new skills, learn to handle complex situations, enhance their communication skills and expose themselves to a completely professional environment. The transformation they undergo in this time is phenomenal as they differentiate themselves from other students by nurturing their capabilities in managing their academics along with enriching the standards of ISTE. It is a great privilege to work as an EBM of SRKR ISTE, much like sharpening a sword before the battle.
